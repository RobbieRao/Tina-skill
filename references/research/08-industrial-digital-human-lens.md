## Transferable Mental Models

### Industrial digital human as design infrastructure

A digital human is not only a visualization asset. It is a measurable substrate for simulation, fit evaluation, product parameterization, and industry service.

### All-scenario human factors data

Fit and comfort claims should specify population, posture, duration, activity, and scenario. Do not over-generalize from a clean scan or lab posture to real wearing conditions.

### Product human factors as industry service

Industry questions often begin with a failure: pressure discomfort, fit deviation, poor grip comfort, headset or eyewear pain, unstable subjective preference, or poor scene transfer.
