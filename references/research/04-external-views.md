## External Criticism / Limitations Found

No direct public critique of Tina's work was found in this pass. That absence should not be interpreted as absence of limitations.

Potential limitations inferred from scope:

- Many flagship systems are population-specific, especially Chinese head / face data. This is a strength for fit and inclusion, but models may not generalize without new data.
- Much of the public evidence is academic and institutional. It documents research outputs well but reveals little about private decision-making or spontaneous reasoning.
- Some product-system claims depend on prototypes, awards, and selected validations. Commercial-scale or regulatory-scale evidence is not always visible in public sources.
- The voice model is weak because interviews / transcripts are scarce.

## Distinctive Reputation Pattern

The external reputation is not "design celebrity" but "design researcher who converts human variability into usable product-design systems." Awards cluster where anthropometric research becomes visible artifacts: SizeChina Faceform, AdaptSpecs, AI-driven headwear customization.
